Our Hygienic Pani Puri

Pani Puri Image - Very Tasty and Crispy

At Tasty Zaika, we take pride in serving the most hygienic and delicious pani puri you'll ever taste! Our pani puri is made with the freshest ingredients, purified water, and follows strict hygiene standards.

Our Brand Story

From a Small Kitchen to Your Favorite Taste

Tasty Zaika began in 2025 with a simple mission: to bring authentic, flavorful Indian street food to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and quality.

Founded by Mrs. Sharma, a passionate home cook with generations of traditional recipes, Tasty Zaika started as a small home kitchen serving neighbors and friends. The overwhelming positive response and love for our food encouraged us to grow into what we are today.

Our name "Zaika" means "taste" or "flavor" in Hindi, and we've stayed true to this meaning by never compromising on the authentic flavors that make Indian street food so special.

Today, we continue to uphold Mrs. Sharma's original recipes while innovating with new flavors and maintaining our commitment to hygiene, quality, and customer satisfaction.
